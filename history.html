THE LIFE OF KANYE:

A MUSICAL HISTORY

Kanye West is most notably known as one of the best rappers/producers alive. From his lyrical genius to his fresh clothing line, there is almost nothing this man cannot do.

Have you ever wondered if his feelings in his lyrics are positive or negative? Well that is what this page is dedicated to. Let's see what makes Kanye West do his Kanye Best.

Take a look at a sentiment analysis of his top 11 songs. These songs are certified multi-platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America® (RIAA). Here is a little key before we go investigate: the middle line represents neutrality, and the left is negative whereas the right is positive.

Kanye West Top 11 Songs Sentiment Analysis

In the analysis above, it is intersting to see the song titles and how relevant they are to the sentiment. Two of his saddest songs, "Love Lockdown" and "Heartless," recieved no Grammy's that year but are still within his top 7 songs of all time.

Another interesting point of this analysis was that the lyrics were analyzed by words and not by sentences. Therefore, songs like "Gold Digger" may have recieved a different score if a different algorithm was used.

Futhermore, two songs, "Ni**gas in Paris" and "All of the Lights" won the most Grammy's out of the bunch and yet are on two different sides of the spectrum.

The R package used for this sentiment analysis was "sentimentr." The lyrics were gathered from AZlyrics.com and from Genius.com.
